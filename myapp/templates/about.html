Creative Furniture Design

AIDW furniture was made of many different materials, including reeds, wood, stone, metals, straws, and ivory.

With the power to deliver customised modular solutions for our showroom partners, mass manufacturing for large projects and manufacturing for our OEM partners, Indoline has amassed expertise over the decades to cater to different customer base and their needs.
